⚔ RIG MAP — the character art template

The blueprint on the left is REED's rest skeleton with every socket and proportion labelled (units: feet at (0,0), +x faces right, y up = negative). Draw your character design over the grid on the right at these proportions, then translate the pieces into a rig spec (public/js/rigs/data/template.rig.js → see RIG_FORMAT.md). The bottom strip is the live rig moving through its states.
