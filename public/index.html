HOW TO FIGHT

⌨ KEYBOARD

  • A / D or ← → — move
  • Space — jump (tap again mid-air = double jump)
  • J — attack  ·  K — special
  • Shift — shield  ·  L — grab
  • S / ↓ while falling — fast-fall
  • Hold J + a direction — charge a smash attack

🎮 CONTROLLER

  • Stick / D-pad — move (smash it sideways to dash)
  • A — attack  ·  B — special
  • X / Y — jump
  • Bumpers / triggers — shield  ·  shield + A — grab
  • Start — pause  ·  navigate menus with the d-pad, A select, B back

📜 THE RULES

  • Direction + attack/special = different moves (up, down, side, neutral)
  • Damage builds % — the higher it climbs, the further you fly
  • Knock rivals past the blast zone to take a stock
  • 3 stocks each. Last legend standing wins.
  • Hold jump for full hops, tap for short hops. Up-special to recover!
  • Fall past the edge? Drift in to grab the ledge — brief invulnerability, then climb, jump, or attack up
  • Grab (L / shield+A) a shielding rival, then a direction to throw — or tap attack to pummel
  • Hold attack on a tilt to charge a smash — release for a devastating hit
  • Crouch (hold down) to crouch-cancel — you take less knockback
  • Esc / Start pauses — resuming counts down 3·2·1 for both players
